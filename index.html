What it does

Calm for them. Peace of mind for you.

Answers in your voice

Record answers to the questions they ask most. Cassi plays your recording every time — never impatient, never a robot when it matters.

The grounding board

Today's date, what's happening, who to expect — always on the screen in large, clear text, easing the evening confusion hours.

Gentle medication check-ins

At dose time, Cassi asks. Saying “I took it” is enough. If a dose is missed, your phone knows — theirs doesn't nag.

“Are you okay?” in one tap

Tap Check In on your phone and Cassi warmly asks. Their answer comes straight back to you.

Calls without the confusion

They say “Cassi, call Sarah” and the tablet rings you on FaceTime. You can ring them back through Cassi, too.

Only the alerts that matter

Your phone buzzes for a missed medication, a check-in answer, or a call request — everything else waits quietly in the app.

Questions

FAQ

What do I need to run Compasse?

An iPad (2017 or newer) for your parent — the one in a drawer is probably perfect — and an iPhone for you. A simple tablet stand or wall mount helps, and the iPad should stay plugged in.

Does my parent need to learn anything?

No. There's nothing to tap, no passwords, no menus on their side. The board is always on; they just talk to it. Setup on their iPad is a one-time code that you enter.

Is this a medical device? Can it call 911?

No. Compasse is a comfort and communication tool, not a medical or emergency device. It must never be relied on for emergencies — always keep your existing emergency plans in place.

What about privacy? Is it always listening?

Conversations happen only when your parent talks to Cassi, and your recordings stay private to your family. Read the full privacy policy — it's written in plain English.

What does it cost?

During our family trial, Compasse is free. At launch it will be a monthly subscription you can cancel anytime — trial families lock in our founding-family price.

Can more than one family member use it?

Today each family has one caregiver phone connected; support for siblings and other family members is on the way.

What if it doesn't work for us?

Tell us — there's a Send Feedback button right on your dashboard and a human answers it. If Compasse isn't a fit, we'll help you offboard and delete your family's data completely.
